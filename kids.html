Kids Martial Arts

The Kids Martial Arts Program is designed for children from ages 7-12. In class, children will learn the martial art of Taekwondo and self-defense. This program will teach your child to set and accomplish goals, boost their socialization skills, and promote non-violent conflict resolution. Your child will increase physical fitness and be better prepared to deal with bullies and adversity in their lives. All of our classes are conducted by our highly-qualified instructors in a safe and controlled environment. The Tigers program is not a prerequisite - anybody can join!

All of our classes have a Zoom option; we currently have students from all over the United States who choose to train with us remotely.

Become a better person physically and mentally,and start your journey to becoming a black belt leader. Try a class today!
