Krav Maga Self Defense

Krav Maga was originally developed for the Israeli army, and is one of the most battle-tested martial arts in the world. At Whitehead's martial arts, you will learn a self-defense system that builds confidence and improve situational awareness. We also cover problem solving and conflict resolution, and you will see tremendous improvements in your overall fitness and flexibility.

Chief Master Whitehead has 30 years of experience as a law enforcement officer. Because of this experience, our program does an excellent job of simulating real-life self-defense scenarios. Try a class today!
